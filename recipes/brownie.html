Impossible Brownie Pie Recipe



Description

Very yummy brownie recipe. Follow the recipe and you won't regret it!

Recipe used for odin project

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 ounces semi-sweet cooking chocolate, melted and cooked
  • 1/2 cup baking mix (e.g. Bisquick)
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 margarine or butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Grease 9-inch pie plate
